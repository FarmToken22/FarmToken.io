About Us

FarmToken is an ad-based mining and referral platform. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Data Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email, password
  • Profile Information: Username, referral code
  • Transaction Information: Mining history, withdrawal records, balance
  • Technical Information: IP address, device information, browser type
  • Usage Information: Login times, platform interactions

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Account creation and management
  • Operating mining and referral systems
  • Processing transactions and completing withdrawals
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Providing customer support
  • Platform improvement and personalization
  • Meeting legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your information to third parties. However, information may be shared in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: Services that help operate the platform, such as Firebase and Google Analytics
  • Legal Requirements: Complying with law or responding to legal processes
  • Security: Preventing fraud or abuse
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers or acquisitions

4. Data Security

We take industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Data transmission with SSL/TLS encryption
  • Password hashing via Firebase Authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access for authorized personnel
  • Secure database storage on Firebase

Note: No system is completely secure. While we take reasonable measures, data transmission over the internet does not guarantee complete security.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Right to know what information we hold about you
  • Correction: Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Right to request deletion of information
  • Portability: Right to transfer information to another service
  • Object: Right to object to specific processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at abdulrohimrire013@gmail.com.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: For account functionality and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Analyzing user behavior with Google Analytics
  • Advertising Cookies: Displaying personalized ads (if applicable)
  • Cookie Settings: Cookies can be managed or disabled in your browser, but this may affect some functionality

7. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain third-party links. These are outside our control and their privacy policies may differ from ours. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If children's information is collected, steps will be taken to delete it.

9. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. When changes occur, the new policy will be posted on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be changed. In case of significant changes, you may be notified via email or notification.
